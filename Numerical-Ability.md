# Numerical Ability — Shortcuts, Tips & Tricks

## 📊 Number System, LCM & HCF

### Key Formulas
- **LCM × HCF = Product of two numbers**
- HCF(a,b) = HCF(a, b-a)
- LCM = (a × b) / HCF(a,b)

### Shortcuts
- **HCF of fractions** = HCF of numerators / LCM of denominators
- **LCM of fractions** = LCM of numerators / HCF of denominators
- **Divisibility by 3**: Sum of digits divisible by 3
- **Divisibility by 4**: Last 2 digits divisible by 4
- **Divisibility by 8**: Last 3 digits divisible by 8
- **Divisibility by 9**: Sum of digits divisible by 9
- **Divisibility by 11**: (Sum of odd pos digits) - (Sum of even pos digits) = 0 or multiple of 11

### Tips
- Always find HCF first, then LCM = (a × b) / HCF
- For word problems, identify if asking for LCM (when to meet again) or HCF (maximum grouping)

## 📈 Percentages

### Quick Tricks
- **x% of y = y% of x** (e.g., 48% of 50 = 50% of 48 = 24)
- **1/2 = 50%, 1/3 = 33.33%, 1/4 = 25%, 1/5 = 20%, 1/8 = 12.5%**
- **% increase** = (New - Old)/Old × 100
- **% decrease** = (Old - New)/Old × 100
- **Successive changes**: Net effect = a + b + ab/100 (for two changes of a% and b%)

### Shortcut: Multiplication by 5, 25, 125
- ×5 = ÷2 × 10 (e.g., 48 × 5 = 24 × 10 = 240)
- ×25 = ÷4 × 100 (e.g., 48 × 25 = 12 × 100 = 1200)
- ×125 = ÷8 × 1000 (e.g., 48 × 125 = 6 × 1000 = 6000)

## 💰 Profit & Loss

### Key Formulas
- **Profit%** = (SP - CP) / CP × 100
- **Loss%** = (CP - SP) / CP × 100
- **SP** = CP × (100 + P%) / 100
- **SP** = CP × (100 - L%) / 100

### Shortcut: Successive Discounts
- Two discounts of a% and b% = a + b - ab/100
- Example: 20% + 30% discount = 20 + 30 - 600/100 = 44% effective discount

### Tip
- If SP is same for two items, one sold at x% profit and other at x% loss → **Always NET LOSS = x²/100 %**

## ⏱️ Time, Speed & Distance

### Key Formulas
- **Speed = Distance / Time**
- **Average Speed** = 2xy / (x+y) [for two equal distances at speeds x and y]
- **Relative Speed (same direction)** = S₁ - S₂
- **Relative Speed (opposite direction)** = S₁ + S₂

### Shortcut: km/hr to m/s
- **km/hr × 5/18 = m/s**
- **m/s × 18/5 = km/hr**

### Train Problems
- **Time to cross a pole** = Length of train / Speed
- **Time to cross a platform** = (Length of train + Length of platform) / Speed
- **Two trains crossing each other** = (Sum of lengths) / Relative speed

## ⏰ Time & Work

### Key Formulas
- **Work = Rate × Time**
- **If A can do work in n days** → A's 1 day work = 1/n
- **A and B together** = 1/A + 1/B per day

### Shortcut: LCM Method (FASTEST)
1. Take LCM of all given times (this = total work)
2. Find each person's efficiency = LCM / their time
3. Combined efficiency = sum of efficiencies
4. Time together = LCM / combined efficiency

### Example
- A does work in 12 days, B in 18 days
- LCM(12,18) = 36 (total work)
- A's efficiency = 36/12 = 3, B's efficiency = 36/18 = 2
- Together = 3+2 = 5, Time = 36/5 = 7.2 days

## 📊 Ratios & Proportion

### Key Concepts
- **a:b = c:d** → ad = bc (cross multiplication)
- **Duplicate ratio** of a:b = a²:b²
- **Triplicate ratio** of a:b = a³:b³
- **Sub-duplicate ratio** = √a:√b

### Tip: Alligation Method
When mixing two quantities at different prices/rates:
```
Cheaper          Dearer
     \          /
      Mean Price
     /          \
(Dearer-Mean)  (Mean-Cheaper)
```
Ratio = (Dearer - Mean) : (Mean - Cheaper)

## 🔢 Averages

### Key Formulas
- **Average = Sum / Count**
- **Sum = Average × Count**
- **Weighted Average** = Σ(wᵢxᵢ) / Σwᵢ

### Shortcut
- If average of n numbers is A, and each number is increased by k → New average = A + k
- New member joining: New avg = Old avg + (new value - old avg) / (n+1)

## 🧪 Mixtures & Allegations

### Alligation Rule
```
Cost₁          Cost₂
     \        /
      Mean Cost
     /        \
(Cost₂-Mean) (Mean-Cost₁)
```
Ratio of mixing = (Cost₂ - Mean) : (Mean - Cost₁)

### Repeated Replacement
After n operations of removing x liters and replacing with water:
**Final concentration = Original × (1 - x/V)^n**

## 🎯 General Tips for Numerical Section
1. **Don't calculate exact** — estimate and eliminate wrong options
2. **Use unit digits** to cross-check answers
3. **Practice mental math** — tables, squares (1-30), cubes (1-15)
4. **Time management** — don't spend >1.5 min on any question
5. **Attempt all** — no negative marking!
